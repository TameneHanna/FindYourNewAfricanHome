About Terzaxis

Founded in 2015, Terzaxis has grown from a small real estate venture in Addis Ababa to a pan-African property management leader. Our journey reflects Africa's growth story - ambitious, innovative, and deeply rooted in community values.

We blend traditional African architectural wisdom with modern property management practices to create spaces that resonate with the continent's diverse cultures while meeting global standards.

Our Mission

To transform Africa's property landscape by creating sustainable, culturally resonant spaces that empower communities and drive economic growth.
